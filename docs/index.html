Built Around Real Client Needs

Developed with direct input from IEEE PES UTM Student Chapter to solve their biggest workshop management challenges.

Pain Point 1: Paperwork & Signatures

Every proposal had to follow a strict template and collect signatures from four different people, often requiring three or more revisions before the first approval.

Our Solution

Committee members create proposals using a standardized digital form. The President reviews and approves in one step, with automatic treasury balance checks preventing overspending before any signature is given.

Pain Point 2: Tracking Committee Performance

Leadership struggled to see who was contributing, who needed more opportunities, and who was underperforming across workshops and tasks.

Our Solution

Tasks can be assigned to committee members with clear deadlines and progress tracking. Performance scores give the President a transparent view of individual contributions over time.

Pain Point 3: Chasing Signatures Separately

Collecting approvals from four people meant contacting different individuals through different channels — slow, fragmented, and easy to lose track of.

Our Solution

All approvals and payment verifications happen inside the app. Committee creates, President approves, Treasurer verifies payments, and Students register — each role notified instantly, no external chasing needed.

Key Features

Everything You Need to Run Workshops

Built specifically for IEEE PES UTM Student Chapter workflows.

Event Registration

Students can browse upcoming workshops, view event details, and register with a simple two-step process including payment proof upload.

Proposal Management

Committee members create detailed proposals with budgets, fees, and signatures. Presidents review and approve with treasury balance checks.

Payment Verification

Treasurers view payment submissions, inspect receipt images, and verify or reject payments with instant status updates.

Task & Performance

Assign tasks to committee members, track progress, and view member performance scores to keep everyone accountable.

Real-time Notifications

Get notified about proposal approvals, payment verifications, task assignments, and upcoming events instantly.

Role-based Access

Four distinct roles — Student, Committee, President, and Treasurer — each with tailored screens and permissions.

About Us

Who We Are

Workshop Wizard is a capstone project developed for the IEEE PES UTM Student Chapter at Universiti Teknologi Malaysia. Our team worked closely with the chapter across four Agile sprints to understand their workshop management pain points and build a mobile solution tailored to their needs.

From proposal submissions and presidential approvals to payment verification and student registrations, every feature was shaped by direct feedback from the club. The result is a clean, role-based Flutter application backed by Firebase, designed to reduce paperwork, improve transparency, and help student organizations run events more efficiently.

Our mission is to give student chapters a modern, easy-to-use platform that saves time, keeps committees aligned, and delivers a smooth experience for every workshop participant.
